Description

SohoLIFF is a ground-breaking film festival bringing together new and seasoned filmmakers in one space – a showcase and networking event where ideas can be shared, and advice proffered. Including drama, documentary, comedy, animation, experimental, horror, student and social impact categories… it is open to everyone.
